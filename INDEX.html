Efficiency Solutions for Shifts Without Clerks:
  • Shared Templates: Quick form/email responses.
  • Tablet-Based Review: Mobile tools to avoid desk time.
  • Voice-to-Text Tools: Fast documentation updates.
  • Prepped Paperwork: 1st shift clerk preps labels/forms.
  • Barcode Integration: Reduce manual data entry.
  • Batch Admin Time: 1–2 periods per shift to handle paperwork.

Shipping Structure

  • 3 Full Supervisors
  • Each shift has 3 Forklift Operators
  • 1st Shift has dedicated Clerk
  • Complex ops: Admin, Fulfillment, CHEP, Corrugated

Proposed Receiving Structure

  • 1 Supervisor
  • 2 Leads (one per night shift)
  • Night shifts focus on Production Support
  • Day shift handles full Receiving Operations
